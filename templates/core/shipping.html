Where We Deliver

We currently deliver across the UK and selected international regions. If your lands are beyond the fog, reach out before ordering and we’ll confirm availability.

  • UK: Standard + Express
  • EU: Tracked International
  • Rest of World: Limited (varies by item)

Processing Times

Orders are picked, checked, and sealed with wax. Most orders leave the Emporium within:

  • Standard: 1–2 business days
  • Peak periods: up to 3 business days
  • Made-to-order / rare finds: noted on product page

Delivery Timeframes

Once dispatched, delivery speed depends on your chosen service:

  • UK Standard: 2–4 business days
  • UK Express: 1–2 business days
  • International Tracked: 5–12 business days

Rates

Rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and parcel weight. As a guide:

  • UK Standard: from £3.95
  • UK Express: from £6.95
  • International Tracked: from £11.95

Packaging

Every order is packed to endure the road — protective wrap, reinforced edges, and tamper-evident seals. Fragile relics receive additional padding.

If your parcel arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos.
