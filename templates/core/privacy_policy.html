1. Who We Are

Ashen Emporium operates as an online retailer of curated goods. For the purposes of data protection law, we act as the data controller for personal information collected through this website.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Name and contact details
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Email address
  • Order history
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our website experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely via third-party payment providers. We do not store full card details on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil orders and comply with accounting and legal requirements.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including UK GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance site functionality and analyse traffic. You may disable cookies via your browser settings.

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact us via the Contact page.
